How I work

I look for the moment behavior changes.

  1. 01

    Reduce ambiguity

    Name the user, the decision, and what evidence would actually change the product direction.

  2. 02

    Watch for friction

    Pay attention to hesitation, overconfidence, workarounds, recovery, and moments of false certainty.

  3. 03

    Separate signal

    Keep observations, interpretations, and recommendations distinct so teams can trust the synthesis.

  4. 04

    Design the next question

    Turn findings into tradeoffs, prototypes, safeguards, or the next study the team should run.

About

I care about the parts of technology people hesitate around.

I am interested in the small moments that decide trust: unclear AI feedback, confusing onboarding, unsafe assumptions, and interfaces that make people feel less capable than they are.
